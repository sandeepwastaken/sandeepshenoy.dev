Portify

A GUI for IndexedDB-based Audio Streaming

Portify lets you stream your own music directly in your browser. Upload your songs, put them into playlists, and enjoy features like shuffling, and more, all powered by your browser's IndexedDB for fast, private, offline-ready storage.

  • Import your audio files into playlists
  • Create, delete, and download playlists as custom .prts
  • Play songs in order or shuffle
  • Offline support with your music staying in your browser
  • Simple & intuitive interface
